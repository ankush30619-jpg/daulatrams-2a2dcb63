Refund & Reimbursement Policy

Fair, transparent and prompt — the way it should be.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are strictly limited to cases involving Quality Issues (Spoilage / Expiry) or Non-Availability of Stock. Refunds will not be issued for "change of mind" or personal taste preferences.

Your Choice: Refund or Exchange

If your claim is approved:

  • Option A (Exchange): We will ship a fresh replacement batch to you immediately via Express Shipping.
  • Option B (Refund): We will credit the full invoice amount back to you.

Refund Timelines

  • Prepaid Orders: The amount will be credited to your original payment method (Card / UPI / Wallet) within 3–5 working days.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Our team will contact you to request your UPI ID or Bank Account details. Once provided, the refund is processed via NEFT within 48 hours.
