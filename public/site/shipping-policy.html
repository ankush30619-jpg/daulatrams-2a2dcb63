Shipping, Delivery & Logistics

Every order is treated as a gift of health — handled with care from Karnal to your doorstep.

Last Updated: January 2026

At Daulatram's (daulatrams.com), operated by Daulat Ram Mukund Lal Swadeshi Kendra, we treat every order as a gift of health. Our logistics network is designed to ensure the premium quality of our products — preserved in our Karnal facility — is maintained until it reaches your doorstep.

Dispatch & Processing

  • The 24-Hour Commitment: All orders placed before 4:00 PM are processed and dispatched within 24 hours. Orders placed on Sundays or national holidays are dispatched on the next working day.
  • Quality Inspection: Before dispatch, every package undergoes a multi-point quality check to ensure seal integrity and freshness.

Shipping Charges & Tiers

  • Premium Shipping (Prepaid): Orders above ₹999 qualify for complimentary (free) shipping.
  • Standard Shipping: For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping fee of ₹50 is applied.
  • Prepaid Advantage: All prepaid orders receive an additional 3% discount automatically at checkout.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): A convenience fee of ₹50 is applicable on all COD orders. COD is restricted to orders with a maximum value of ₹2000.

Delivery Timelines

  • Metros & Tier 1 Cities: 3–5 working days.
  • Rest of India: 7–10 working days.
  • Tracking: Upon dispatch, a tracking link from logistics partners (BlueDart, Delhivery, or Ecom Express) will be sent via SMS and Email.

Non-Serviceable Zones & Delays

While we ship to 19,000+ pin codes across India, certain remote areas may be unserviceable. If your location is outside our delivery zone, you will be notified immediately.

Delivery may be delayed due to "Force Majeure" events such as extreme weather, strikes, or regional lockdowns.
