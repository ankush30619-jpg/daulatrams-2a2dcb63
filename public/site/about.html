Our Heritage

Trust the Legacy of Daulatram's

For generations, Daulatram's has been a name of trust in Ayurveda, purity and wellness. What began decades ago with the vision of Ayurvedic doctor Shri Daulat Ram Ji in the bustling lanes of Sarafa Bazar, Karnal, has today grown into a holistic brand that blends ancient wisdom with modern care.

"True health comes from living in harmony with nature."

Carrying the legacy forward, Mr. Mukund Arora brought modern manufacturing, rigorous quality standards and FSSAI certification to the family's time-tested formulations — without ever compromising the purity at their heart.

Every product we create is crafted with authenticity, care and precision in our own facilities at Karnal and Yamuna Nagar — never outsourced, never diluted.

Our Journey

From One Shop to Every Indian Home

The Beginning

A Doctor's Vision

Shri Daulat Ram Ji, a practising Ayurvedic doctor, opens Daulat Ram Mukund Lal Swadeshi Kendra in Sarafa Bazar, Karnal — preparing classical formulations by hand for his patients.

Second Generation

A Name Built on Trust

The family shop becomes a Karnal institution. Recipes are refined and passed down; customers travel from across Haryana for Daulatram's ghee, honey and herbal preparations.

Modern Era

Own Manufacturing

Mr. Mukund Arora establishes dedicated manufacturing facilities in Karnal and Yamuna Nagar — bringing FSSAI certification, GMP practices and lab testing to every batch.

Today

Ayurveda for Every Home

Daulatram's ships to 19,000+ pincodes across India, serving 50,000+ families with the same purity that started it all — now a click away.
